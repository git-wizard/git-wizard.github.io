A brief explanation of what our project was to achieve.

### An explanation of our project:

Our project was to see if we could re-create a working battery which, potentially, could power at least a light bulb. We were able to power an LED, however, we were not able to power a ligh bulb therefore did not even try powering anything larger. Though we wish we could do so. The battery we created was made using lemons. To be able to do so we researched a few methods for creating batteries and found that it was very hard to power anything larger than an LED, we did not stop there however, we continued to research methods of creating a battery, all were out of our reach.

### Our Method for creating a lemon battery:

1- We were to retrieve a few resources from our teacher.

Needed Items:
  • 1) Lemons
  • 2) Zinc
  • 3) Copper
  • 4) Wires with crocodile clips
  • 5) Voltmeteter

2- We inserted, on one end of the lemon, a piece of zinc; on the other a piece of copper.

3- We then clipped on two wires, on on either side, to the copper and zinc.

4- The other end of both wires was inserted into the voltmeter to test the voltage.

### Result:

We managed to get around 0.9V of electricity from one lemon, using two; we were able to power an LED, however, it was not very bright. We then used 3 instead, which worked a lot better. (This part of the experiment was not done at school)
